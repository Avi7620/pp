Urkund - Academic Integrity Software

Urkund is like a detective for academic integrity. It's a software tool used by educational institutions to detect plagiarism in student assignments and research papers. With Urkund, teachers can upload documents, and the software compares them against a vast database of academic sources to identify any similarities. It helps ensure that students are submitting original work and promotes honesty and fairness in academic settings. Think of it as a watchdog that keeps academic integrity in check.
