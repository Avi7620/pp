OpenEHR - Electronic Health Records

OpenEHR is like a blueprint for organizing and storing health information in electronic records. It helps doctors and hospitals keep track of patient data in a flexible and standardized way. By using OpenEHR, healthcare systems can communicate better and adapt easily to changes, making it easier to share and use medical information across different software and devices.
