R - Statistical Analysis Software

R is a programming language and software environment commonly used for statistical analysis and data visualization. It helps people crunch numbers, find patterns in data, and make sense of information. It's popular because it's powerful, flexible, and has a wide range of tools for analyzing all sorts of data.
