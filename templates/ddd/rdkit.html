RDKit - Cheminformatics Toolkit

RDKit is an open-source toolkit for cheminformatics, primarily used for cheminformatics and drug discovery tasks. It includes functionalities such as chemical structure representation, structure similarity searching, substructure searching, chemical fingerprinting, molecular descriptors calculation, and conformer generation and molecular alignment.
