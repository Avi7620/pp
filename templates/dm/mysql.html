MySQL - Relational Database Management System

MySQL is like a digital filing cabinet for storing and managing data. It's a popular open-source relational database management system that helps you organize, retrieve, and manipulate data efficiently. With MySQL, you can create databases to store different types of information, from customer records to product catalogs. It's widely used in web development, business applications, and many other areas where data management is essential.
