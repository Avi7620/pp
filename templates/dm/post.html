PostgreSQL - Relational Database Management System

PostgreSQL is like a smart filing cabinet for storing and managing data. It's a powerful open-source relational database management system (RDBMS) that helps you organize and retrieve information efficiently. With PostgreSQL, you can store data in tables, query it using a structured language called SQL, and ensure data integrity and security. It's a popular choice for applications that require robust and scalable data storage, from small businesses to large enterprises.
